05 — The Vocabulary of Authority

We don't ask for permission. We consult.

The language a seller uses signals their status. We eliminate the low-status markers that give away the pitch — and replace them with the frame of a peer.

Kill list — never say

  • "I'd love to get on your calendar."
  • "Does that make sense?"
  • "I'm just checking in."
  • "Thank you for your time."

Calibrate — say instead

  • "My calendar is open Tuesday to review the math."
  • "Based on what you've deployed, here's where the friction lies."
  • "The next tactical step is a technical audit."
  • "I appreciate the insights you shared."
